About Me

I am a third-year B.Des student from IDC School of Design, IIT Bombay. I enjoy interaction design and user research. Being the Design Head of the Institute's student media body, I've learned what it is to work on real-life design projects in a team. I am always open to new opportunities that give me a chance to grow as a designer. I enjoy volunteering and have worked as a design intern with Child Rights and You NGO. I spend time reading about psychology and astronomy and I'm a huge fan of Formula 1 racing. Thank you for visiting my portfolio :)
